About us

Welcome to Good Gums!

The place where you can find native Australian plants for your garden.

We absolutely love all things gardening.

Our specialty is in providing high-quality Australian plants that, and the knowledge needed to grow them in your very own backyard

In recent years, Australian plants and flowers have been gaining popularity due to their unique colour palette and that they are designed to grow in our - often harsh - Australian environment.

At Good Gums, we offer the highest quality Australian plants for the comsumer, including delivery and advice.

If you're not familiar with growing any of the trees or plants you wish to purchase, don't worry! Our team will be able to advise you on all the planting and maintenance information you need to be successful
